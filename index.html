FAQ / 07

Questions, answered honestly.

Don't see yours? Email me directly — I reply within a day.

How long until I see real SEO results?

Quick wins from technical fixes can land in 2–4 weeks. Meaningful traffic and ranking lifts typically begin in months 3–4, with compounding gains from month 6 onward. I'd be skeptical of anyone promising faster.

Do you guarantee first-page Google rankings?

No ethical SEO consultant can — Google's algorithm is a black box and competitor behavior shifts daily. What I do guarantee: a defensible strategy, transparent reporting, and the same discipline that's gotten 80+ projects to page one.

Why hire a freelance SEO expert instead of an agency?

You get senior-level work without agency overhead, account managers, or 12-month lock-ins. Direct Slack access, fixed pricing, faster turnarounds. Agencies still make sense for very large enterprise scopes — but for most founders, a consultant ships more, faster.

Which industries do you typically work with?

B2B SaaS, D2C ecommerce, healthcare, education, and local services dominate my client list. I avoid industries I can't add a real edge to (gambling, adult, crypto pump-and-dumps). If you're unsure — just ask.

What does an engagement actually look like?

Three formats: a one-time audit (2–3 weeks), a project sprint (8–12 weeks), or a monthly retainer (month-to-month, no lock-in). Every engagement starts with a paid audit so we both know what we're walking into.

How do you measure SEO success?

Rankings are an input. Real KPIs are pipeline-level: organic-attributed signups, MQLs, revenue, and cost-per-acquisition vs paid. Every client gets a live Looker dashboard tying SEO to the metrics your CFO cares about.
